Who are we and what is the mission

When you think about the world...what do you see? Do you see a collection of static, immovable, unchangeble objects? Or do you see something that is built by people, but only by and for those that are willing to or have the capability to create. The latter is what we would like to see in our people as it is important to understand the malle0ability of this world in order to be able to change it.
